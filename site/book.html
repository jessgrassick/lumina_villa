Direct Booking

Why book direct?

Booking direct means your money goes further and your stay goes smoother.

No platform fees

Airbnb charges guests a 14 to 20% service fee on top of the nightly rate. Booking direct means you pay the actual rate, nothing more. On a week's stay, that is a meaningful saving.

Direct communication

When you book direct, you are talking to the team that manages the villa from day one. Not a customer service bot. Not a regional office. The people who will stock your fridge, arrange your chef, and meet you at the airport.

Flexibility on requests

Need to arrive at noon? Want to extend by a day? Arranging a proposal and need help keeping it secret? Platform bookings are rigid. Direct bookings give us room to work with you.

Best available rate, always

We never offer lower rates through third parties than we do direct. Book direct and you have the best rate available. Simple.

We are also on Airbnb and Expedia if you prefer the security of a platform. Links available when live. Either way, the villa is the same. The team is the same.

Booking questions answered

Yes. Direct WhatsApp booking is the standard in Bali and has been for years. Over 90% of private villa bookings on the island happen this way. It is not a workaround; it is how the industry operates.

At Lumina Villa, we provide a written booking confirmation before you transfer any payment. You receive your booking reference, a deposit receipt, check-in instructions, and our cancellation policy in writing. We are also listed on Airbnb and Expedia for guests who prefer platform protection. Both options are valid. Direct booking simply removes the fees and puts you in direct contact with the team from the start.

Our cancellation policy is straightforward. Cancel 30 or more days before check-in and you receive a full refund. Cancel 15 to 29 days before check-in and you receive a 50% refund. Cancellations within 14 days of check-in are non-refundable.

That said, we are genuinely flexible. Travel plans fall apart for reasons that have nothing to do with wanting to leave: illness, flights, family situations. If your circumstances change, message us before you formally cancel. We will do what we can, whether that is moving your dates, holding a credit, or finding another arrangement. We would rather have a conversation than apply a policy.

Payment is via international bank transfer or PayPal. When we confirm your booking, we send you the payment details along with your quote.

A 50% deposit secures the booking. You receive a deposit receipt and written confirmation as soon as it clears. The remaining 50% is due 14 days before check-in. We send a reminder at that point. No surprises, no late fees.

Standard check-in is at 3:00 PM. Standard check-out is at 11:00 AM. This gives our housekeeping team time to prepare the villa properly between guests.

Early check-in from 12:00 PM is often available, depending on the previous guests' departure. If you are landing at midday and want to head straight to the villa, let us know when you book and we will do our best to accommodate. Late check-out can sometimes be arranged for a small additional fee. Just ask. Our complimentary airport pickup is timed to your actual arrival, so even if the villa is not ready, we can store your luggage and get you settled with a welcome drink.

The grocery stocking service can also be tailored. If you need particular products (baby food, specific dietary items, certain brands), include them in your list and we will source them before you arrive. Just let us know when you send your enquiry and we will note everything against your booking.

Small private celebrations, yes. We have hosted birthday styling setups, anniversary dinners, and proposals at the villa, all beautifully. What we do not host: large parties, events with external guests, or anything that would impact the neighbourhood. Jl. Babakan Kubu is a quiet residential street.
