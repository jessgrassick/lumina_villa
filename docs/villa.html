Shared spaces

The parts of the villa everyone uses.

And everyone enjoys.

Open-plan living + dining

The living and dining area flows as one large room: no awkward divisions between where you eat and where you sit. Rattan furniture, ceiling fans, and doors that open fully to the terrace so the inside and outside blend. The dining table seats six, with cutlery and tableware for 10+. Invite friends from nearby villas over for a private chef dinner. The best nights in Bali end around your table.

Full kitchen

A proper working kitchen, not a kitchenette. Full-size fridge, four-burner gas stove, microwave, coffee maker, blender, rice cooker, and a full set of cookware and utensils for 10+ guests. Stock it from the Pererenan morning market and cook Balinese ingredients at home. Or let our private chef take over for the night.

Pool terrace

The heart of the villa. The pool runs along the terrace edge, with the rice fields beyond. Six sun loungers with cushions, a shaded sitting area, and enough deck space to spread out without everyone being on top of each other. The pool faces west and catches full afternoon sun. Golden hour by the water every day.

Outdoor bathtub

A freestanding stone bathtub set into the garden at the edge of the terrace, surrounded by tropical planting. It's the thing guests mention most in reviews. Use it at sunrise with coffee, or fill it at night with the garden lights on. The concierge can arrange flower petals and salts on request.

Garden

A mature tropical garden wraps the villa on three sides. Frangipani, palms and dense green planting keep it shaded and private. The garden is maintained daily and kept free of insect concerns.

Upstairs deck

A shaded deck on the upper level with seating for six. The rice fields stretch out in front of you, uninterrupted, for half a kilometre. No construction cranes, no neighbouring rooftops. Just green. It's the afternoon drinks spot. The shade cover means you get the view without the sun in your eyes.

House rules

A quiet, residential setting.

A few simple things to keep it that way.

  • Check-in: 3:00 PM / Check-out: 11:00 AM

    Early check-in and late check-out can often be arranged. Just ask the concierge in advance. We will do our best.

  • No smoking indoors

    Smoking is permitted on the outdoor terrace and in the garden. Please dispose of cigarettes responsibly.

  • No pets

    We love animals, but Bali's wildlife, planting and pool surrounds are not suited to them. No pets at the villa.

  • Quiet hours: 10 PM to 8 AM

    The villa sits on a working agricultural lane. Rice field workers and neighbouring families are up early. Loud music and gatherings after 10 PM are not permitted.

  • Maximum 6 guests overnight

    The villa sleeps six. Day visitors are welcome; overnight guests are limited to those on the booking.
